What is
Nashaty?

Nashaty was created to solve a fundamental problem for modern parents: how to help their children grow in a world full of digital distractions.

Mission

To be the one central place families in the MENA region turn to enrich their children's time, empowering parents to discover high-quality opportunities, build skills, and celebrate the unique journey of childhood

Vision

Helping parents to turn their children's free time into developmental opportunities
